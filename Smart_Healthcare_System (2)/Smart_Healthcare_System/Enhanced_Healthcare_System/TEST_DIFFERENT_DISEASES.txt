the ML model was trained with. The model expects symptoms in this EXACT order:

2. Hollow
3. cold and cough

5. fever
6. chest pain
7. breathing problem
8. Throat pain
9. head pain
10. stomach pain
11. diarrhea
12. omitting
13. back pain
14. Swollen feet

The fix ensures symptoms are always sent to the model in this exact order.
