🏃‍♂️ Weight Loss Diet Plan वजन कमी करण्याची आहार योजना
Low Carb कमी कर्ब
Designed to reduce body weight by controlling carbohydrate intake, increasing proteins, and encouraging thermogenesis. Excellent for managing lipid profile and visceral fat. कार्बोहायड्रेट नियंत्रण, प्रथिने वाढवणे आणि उष्णजनन (thermogenesis) द्वारे शरीराचे वजन कमी करण्यासाठी डिझाइन केलेले. लिपिड प्रोफाइल आणि व्हिसेरल फॅट व्यवस्थापनासाठी उत्तम.
🌿 Vegetarian Plan
🍳 Breakfast
Tofu scramble with spinach & mushrooms, unsweetened green tea, and a small bowl of papaya.
🍲 Lunch
Boiled paneer salad with cucumbers, bell peppers, tomatoes, chickpeas, and olive oil dressing.
🥜 Snack
A handful of almonds (8–10) or dry roasted makhana (foxnuts).
🥗 Dinner
Sautéed broccoli, beans, and carrots with grilled paneer/tofu blocks. No rice or wheat.
🍗 Non-Vegetarian Plan
🍳 Breakfast
Veggie omelette (2 eggs) with spinach, unsweetened green tea, and a boiled egg white on the side.
🍲 Lunch
Grilled chicken breast (150g) over a large salad with cucumbers, bell peppers, tomatoes, and lemon dressing.
🥜 Snack
Hard boiled egg (1) or tuna strips with cucumber sticks. Avoid any fried snacks.
🥗 Dinner
Grilled salmon (120g) with sautéed broccoli, beans, and carrots. No rice/wheat bread.
