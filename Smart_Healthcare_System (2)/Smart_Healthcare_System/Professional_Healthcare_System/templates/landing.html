Everything You Need in One Platform

From AI diagnosis to digital prescriptions, we've built the complete healthcare toolkit for modern medical practice.

AI Disease Prediction

Our machine learning model analyzes 14 symptoms to predict diseases with over 94% accuracy. Get instant, reliable diagnoses powered by trained medical data.

Advanced Analytics Dashboard

Doctors get a real-time analytics hub with 6 interactive charts, live patient feeds, KPI counters, and age-disease distribution maps.

Digital Prescriptions
